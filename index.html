MyExpenseTrail Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

MyExpenseTrail respects your privacy. This app is designed to help users record and manage personal expense information securely and privately.

Information We Collect

MyExpenseTrail may store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Expense entries (amount, date, category)
  • Optional notes entered by the user
  • Optional receipt images selected by the user
  • Optional profile information (e.g. name or reference label)

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers
  • Contact details
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising data
  • Financial account login details

How Your Data Is Used

All data entered into MyExpenseTrail is used solely to:

  • Display expense records within the app
  • Generate summaries and totals
  • Allow the user to export their own data (CSV or PDF) for personal use, including sharing with an accountant or tax professional

Data Storage

All expense data is stored locally on your device using secure system storage. MyExpenseTrail does not transmit, upload, sync, or back up data to any external servers.

The developer does not have access to your data.

Data Sharing

MyExpenseTrail does not share data with any third parties.

Any data sharing (such as exporting a CSV or PDF file) is initiated explicitly by the user.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Users may delete individual records at any time
  • Users may delete all stored data within the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from the device

Third-Party Services

MyExpenseTrail does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If in-app purchases are enabled, they are processed securely by Apple or Google. The developer does not receive or store any personal payment information.

Financial Disclaimer

MyExpenseTrail is intended for personal expense tracking and organisational purposes only. It does not provide financial, tax, or legal advice. Users are responsible for verifying any exported data before submitting it to accountants or tax authorities.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be published at this URL.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via the App Store or Google Play listing.
